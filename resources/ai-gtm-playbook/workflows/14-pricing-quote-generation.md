# 14. Pricing & Quote Generator

**Funnel Stage**: Conversion
**Time Saved**: 1–2 hours per quote
**Tools**: Claude + Google Sheets / Notion
**Difficulty**: Beginner

---

## What This Workflow Does

Generates a professional, customised pricing proposal based on the client's requirements discussed in the meeting. Removes the friction of starting from a blank page — AI produces a structured, transparent quote you can review and send within 30 minutes.

---

## The Prompt Template

```
You are a pricing strategist helping me write a client quote.

My business:
- Company: [YOUR COMPANY]
- Services offered: [LIST YOUR SERVICES/PACKAGES]
- Standard pricing: [YOUR PRICING TIERS — rough ranges are fine]

Client situation:
- Client: [COMPANY NAME]
- What they need: [REQUIREMENTS from discovery meeting]
- Company size: [SIZE — relevant for pricing]
- Budget signal: [What they hinted at, or "unknown"]
- Decision timeline: [WHEN THEY NEED TO DECIDE]

Produce a professional quote with:
1. A 2-sentence intro addressing them by name
2. Scope of work — exactly what is included (and what is not included, to manage expectations)
3. Pricing table — clear line items with individual costs and a total
4. Optional add-ons — 2–3 upsell options they can add later
5. What's included in the investment (support, revisions, access, etc.)
6. Payment terms (suggested: 50% upfront, 50% on completion — or split by milestone)
7. Quote validity — 30 days is standard
8. A one-line note about HRD Corp / SBL-Khas eligibility if applicable

Tone: Professional and clear. No fluff. Let the scope and price speak for itself.
```

---

## Malaysia-Specific Tips

- **Price in RM** — never quote in USD to Malaysian clients unless they explicitly operate in USD
- **GST/SST**: Clarify in the quote whether pricing is inclusive or exclusive of SST (6%)
- **HRD Corp**: If your service qualifies, add a line: "This programme is eligible for SBL-Khas claim under HRD Corp — your company can recover this cost from your levy balance."
- **Negotiation culture**: Malaysian clients often expect a small negotiation — build a 5–10% buffer into your initial quote
