# 06. LinkedIn DM Outreach Sequence

**Funnel Stage**: Interest
**Time Saved**: 2–4 hours per 20 prospects
**Tools**: Claude + LinkedIn (Sales Navigator optional)
**Difficulty**: Beginner–Intermediate

---

## What This Workflow Does

Generates personalised LinkedIn connection requests and a 3-step DM sequence for each prospect. More personal than cold email and ideal for Malaysian B2B where relationship-first culture makes warm outreach more effective than hard pitches.

---

## The Prompt Template

```
You are a LinkedIn outreach specialist for Malaysian B2B sales.

Write a LinkedIn DM outreach sequence for the following prospect:

My background:
- Name: [YOUR NAME]
- Company: [COMPANY]
- Offer: [WHAT YOU SELL]

Prospect details:
- Name: [FIRST NAME]
- Title: [TITLE]
- Company: [COMPANY]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- Something specific from their profile: [recent post / shared connection / career milestone / company news]

Write 4 messages:

Message 1 — Connection Request (300 character limit, no pitch):
Reference something genuine from their profile. Ask to connect as a peer, not as a vendor.

Message 2 — First DM after connecting (send 2 days after they accept):
Thank them for connecting. Share one piece of value (an insight, article, or observation) relevant to their role. No ask.

Message 3 — Value DM (5 days after Message 2):
Share a second piece of value — a data point, a short story about a similar company, or a question that shows you understand their world. Optional soft mention of your offer only if naturally relevant.

Message 4 — Soft Ask (7 days after Message 3):
Ask a simple, low-friction question. Goal is a reply, not a meeting. "Would it be useful if I shared X?" or "Is [TOPIC] something your team is thinking about this year?"

Rules:
- Use first name only
- No pitching in Messages 1–2
- Maximum 4 messages total — do not follow up beyond this
- Tone: Peer-to-peer, not vendor-to-prospect
- Malaysian professional tone: slightly more formal than Western LinkedIn DMs
- Do not use: "I came across your profile", "I hope this message finds you well"
```

---

## Example Input

```
My name: [YOUR NAME], [YOUR COMPANY NAME] ([what you do])
Prospect: Rashdan Yusof, Head of L&D at Maybank (just posted about employee upskilling)
Something specific: He posted 3 days ago about the challenge of keeping training relevant in a fast-changing environment
```

---

## Example Output

**Connection Request**: "Rashdan — your post on keeping training relevant resonated. We work on a similar challenge with L&D teams across Malaysian financial services. Would be good to connect."

**Message 2**: "Thanks for connecting, Rashdan. Saw a stat this week that caught my attention: Malaysian employers say AI is their #1 skills gap but fewer than 30% have run structured AI training for leaders yet. Thought it might be relevant given what you shared. Hope Q2 is treating you well."

---

## Malaysia-Specific Tips

- **LinkedIn penetration in KL corporate world is high** but activity is lower than Western markets — responses may take 3–5 days
- **Shared connections matter**: Always mention if you have a mutual connection — warm path to yes
- **Public holidays**: Do not send DMs on major Malaysian public holidays
- **EO network**: If prospect is also an EO member, mention it — instant credibility
