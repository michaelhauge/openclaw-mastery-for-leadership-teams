# 04. 30-Day Social Media Calendar Generator

**Funnel Stage**: Awareness
**Time Saved**: 4–6 hours per month
**Tools**: Claude / ChatGPT + Canva + Buffer / Hootsuite
**Difficulty**: Beginner

---

## What This Workflow Does

Generates a complete 30-day social media content calendar with post ideas, copy, and image prompts for each platform. Run it once a month and your social presence runs on autopilot.

---

## The Prompt Template

```
You are a social media strategist for Malaysian B2B businesses.

Create a 30-day social media content calendar for:

Business: [COMPANY NAME]
Industry: [INDUSTRY]
Offer: [WHAT YOU SELL]
Target audience: [WHO — job title, industry, location]
Platforms: [LinkedIn / Facebook / Instagram / TikTok — pick what applies]
Brand voice: [Professional / Friendly / Expert / Bold]
Monthly theme: [OPTIONAL — e.g. "AI in manufacturing", "Year-end planning", "Q1 growth"]

For each of the 30 days, provide:
1. Platform
2. Content type (educational / behind-the-scenes / social proof / promotional / engagement question)
3. Post copy (ready to publish — no editing needed)
4. Image prompt for Canva or DALL-E (what the visual should show)
5. Best posting time (KL timezone)

Rules:
- Maximum 20% promotional posts — the rest should be genuinely useful
- Include 2–3 posts per week in Bahasa Malaysia (for Facebook/Instagram reach)
- Include at least 4 posts using Malaysian cultural moments (local public holidays, industry events)
- No generic "Happy Monday!" posts
- Each post must have a clear reason to exist — what does the reader gain?
```

---

## Example Input

```
Business: Suntech Engineering (industrial equipment supplier, Shah Alam)
Industry: Manufacturing / Industrial
Offer: Pneumatic and hydraulic equipment for Malaysian factories
Target audience: Factory managers, maintenance engineers, procurement managers
Platforms: LinkedIn + Facebook
Brand voice: Expert but approachable
Monthly theme: Preventive maintenance reduces downtime
```

---

## Malaysia-Specific Tips

- **Facebook is dominant** for Malaysian SME audiences — do not neglect it for LinkedIn-only
- **Hari Raya, CNY, Deepavali, Merdeka**: Plan posts 2 weeks ahead for major holidays
- **BM content**: Facebook BM posts often outperform English for mass-market Malaysian audiences
- **Posting times**: LinkedIn peaks 8–10am weekdays; Facebook peaks 12–2pm and 7–9pm
