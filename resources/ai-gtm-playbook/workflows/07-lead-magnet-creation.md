# 07. Lead Magnet Creator

**Funnel Stage**: Interest
**Time Saved**: 8–15 hours per lead magnet
**Tools**: Claude + Canva / Google Docs
**Difficulty**: Beginner–Intermediate

---

## What This Workflow Does

Generates complete lead magnets — ebooks, checklists, calculators, report templates — that you offer in exchange for contact details. A strong lead magnet turns cold traffic into warm leads automatically.

---

## The Prompt Template

```
You are a B2B content strategist creating a lead magnet for a Malaysian business.

Create a [TYPE: checklist / ebook outline / guide / calculator template / email templates] on the topic: [TOPIC]

Business context:
- Company: [COMPANY NAME]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- Offer: [WHAT YOU SELL]
- Target reader: [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY TYPE] in Malaysia
- Their biggest problem: [PAIN POINT]

Lead magnet requirements:
- Title: Compelling, specific, numbers where possible (e.g. "The 12-Point AI Readiness Checklist for Malaysian HR Teams")
- Length: [SPECIFY — e.g. 1-page checklist / 8-page guide / 5-email sequence]
- Format: Practical and actionable — every item should be something they can use today
- Malaysian context: Include references to local regulations, tools, or market conditions where relevant
- Branding: Add a footer: "Brought to you by [COMPANY NAME] | [WEBSITE]"
- CTA at end: One clear next step — schedule a call / download another resource / reply to this email

Write the complete content, ready to paste into Canva or Google Docs.
```

---

## Example Input

```
Type: Checklist
Topic: AI readiness for Malaysian leadership teams
Company: [YOUR COMPANY NAME]
Offer: AI training workshops, SBL-Khas eligible
Target reader: HR Managers at mid-market KL companies (200–1,000 staff)
Pain point: Not sure where to start with AI training or how to justify the spend
```

---

## Example Output (partial)

**Title**: The 12-Point AI Readiness Checklist for Malaysian Leadership Teams (2026 Edition)

**Section 1 — Awareness**
- [ ] Does your senior leadership team understand what AI can and cannot do? (Not marketing claims — real capabilities)
- [ ] Have you identified the top 3 tasks your leaders spend time on that AI could assist with?
- [ ] Do your leaders use any AI tools currently (ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude)? Even informally?

**Section 2 — Readiness**
- [ ] Does your company have an AI usage policy? (Especially important for regulated industries)
- [ ] Is your company registered with HRD Corp? (Training costs may be claimable under SBL-Khas)
- [ ] Have you checked your current HRD Corp levy balance?

---

## Malaysia-Specific Tips

- **HRD Corp angle**: A checklist that includes "check your HRD Corp balance" gives HR managers an immediate action item — and positions your training as the natural next step
- **Regulated industries**: Checklists for banking, insurance, and healthcare clients should reference BNM/Bank Negara, SC, or MOH guidelines
- **Bahasa Malaysia version**: For SME and government audiences, consider a BM version — Canva makes translation easy
