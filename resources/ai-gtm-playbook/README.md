## Quick Wins: Where to Start

If you're new to AI GTM tools, start with these four. Each takes less than 30 minutes to set up and delivers a result you can use the same day:

1. **[Workflow 19 — ICP Builder](./workflows/19-icp-builder.md):** Define exactly who your best customers are. This makes everything else sharper.
2. **[Workflow 12 — Sales Call Prep Brief](./workflows/12-sales-call-prep-brief.md):** Walk into every sales call knowing more than you've ever known before.
3. **[Workflow 05 — Cold Email Outreach](./workflows/05-cold-email-outreach.md):** Generate a personalised 4-email sequence for any prospect in under 5 minutes.
4. **[Workflow 04 — Social Media Calendar](./workflows/04-social-media-calendar.md):** One prompt. Thirty days of content. Done.

## Level 1 vs Level 2

**Level 1 — Automation:** AI executes tasks you define. You give it instructions; it follows them. Saves 8–12 hours per week. That's workflows 01–18.

**Level 2 — Intelligence:** AI makes decisions, finds leads, and adapts in real-time without you telling it what to do. Workflows 19–24. This is where the 10x lift comes from.

Start with Level 1. Win fast. Then layer in Level 2 as your confidence grows.

## Malaysia-Specific Notes

This playbook is written for Southeast Asian business owners, with specific attention to:
- **HRD Corp / SBL-Khas:** Malaysia's mandatory training levy system — a powerful sales angle for training and upskilling services
- **WhatsApp-first culture:** Malaysian professionals prefer WhatsApp over email for business communication. Multiple workflows include WhatsApp-specific guidance.
- **Bilingual market:** Bahasa Malaysia tips included where relevant for government and GLC audiences
- **PDPA compliance:** Data privacy guidance included in outreach workflows
